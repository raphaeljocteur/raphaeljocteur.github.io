Inari

Characteristic

  • Game type : 3D platformer / Puzzle
  • References : Portal / Zelda BOTW / Ratchet & Clank : a crack in time
  • Target : Core Gamer
  • Duration : 12 weeks
  • Release : 2019
  • Language : C#
  • Engine : Unity

Description

A dark technology powered by a mysterious energy is corumpting human-beings soul, as Inari, last kami alive, your mission is to climb the tower where the mysterious energy source is kept. Pass multiple enigmatic rooms, use your duplication powers to establish a delayed solo cooperation gameplay, and discover the majesty of this evil tower.

What did i do ?

Camera

In this 3rd person game we needed a camera wo could see all around the player so we choose to make a camera inspired by Zelda Breath of the wild using cinemachine

Physicals elements

We need pushables and pullables boxes, they could also be stackables on top of each other. My job was to ensure that the physics works correctly on cases like push against a wall, pull a stacked box on top of one another, etc .

Character Controls

Since i made the camera, i need to make the character control work with it, if you push your joystick forward you needed to go in the camera direction. I also made basic control like jump and animation controller.

Camera Feedback

When you activate button or events like opening door, we needed to show what change in the world. To do so i made the camera travel to the destination that needed to be showed and come back to the player at the end.
